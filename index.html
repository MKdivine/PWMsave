Why it matters

What makes a display eye-friendly?

🔆

Flicker-Free Backlight

DC dimming instead of PWM eliminates the imperceptible flicker that causes eye fatigue and headaches.

🌙

Low Blue Light Mode

Hardware-level blue light filters reduce harmful short-wave blue light without a yellow tint.

🖥️

Anti-Glare Coating

Matte panels diffuse reflections so ambient light doesn't cause glare or reduce contrast.

🎨

Accurate Color & Contrast

IPS and OLED panels maintain natural colors at wide viewing angles, reducing eye adjustment strain.
